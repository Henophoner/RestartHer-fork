## Project Description

RestartHer is an AI-powered platform designed to help women who have taken long career gaps. It provides personalized guidance by assessing interests and skills and generating a roadmap to restart their professional journey.

---

## The Problem Statement

Many women face challenges returning to the workforce after long career breaks due to outdated skills, lack of guidance, and uncertainty about career paths. Traditional career guidance lacks personalization and actionable steps.

---

## The Solution

RestartHer solves this by providing:

* Personalized authentication to securely identify users
* Surveys to understand skills, interests, and career goals
* AI-driven roadmap generation suggesting step-by-step actions to restart careers

**Technologies/Components Used**

## Features

* **User Authentication:** Secure login and profile creation
* **Skill & Interest Survey:** Collects detailed data for personalized guidance
* **AI-Powered Roadmap:** Generates a career restart plan tailored to each user
* **Progress Tracking:** Allows users to track milestones and achievements
